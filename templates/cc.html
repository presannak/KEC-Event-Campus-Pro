Convention Center

About

The Kongu Convention Centre located at Erode in Tamil Nadu, India consists of a multipurpose hall which is one of the largest in India. The building has the facilities for Conference halls , Seminar halls , Training centre , Guest rooms and VIP rooms , Offices , High-tech Audio Visual facilities for Global conferencing , Cafeteria , Offices etc. The construction completed in July 2010 with a built up area of 18,600 sq metres.

Capacity

The Kongu Convention Centre have a massive capacity of 4500 persons.
