Sir C.V.Raman Seminar Hall

About

The Sir C.V.Raman Seminar Hall is located at Kongu engineering college, Perundurai, Erode in Tamil Nadu, India. It is situated in ground floor of Science and Humanities Block. This hall is used for conducting guest lectures, exciting events, etc.

Capacity

The Sir C.V.Raman Seminar Hall have a capacity of 300 persons.
