Srinivasa Ramanujam Hall

About

The Srinivasa Ramanujam Hall is located at Kongu engineering college, Perundurai, Erode in Tamil Nadu, India. It is situated in ground floor of IT Park and known as Seminar Hall-1. This hall is used for conducting guest lectures, admission purposes, etc.

Capacity

The Srinivasa Ramanujam Hall have a massive capacity of 250 persons.
